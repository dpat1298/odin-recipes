Zucchini and Stuffing Casserole

Description

This dish is super delish.

Ingredients

  • 3 zucchinis sliced into half moons
  • 6oz box of chicken stovetop stuffing
  • 3 Tbsp of melted butter
  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 10.5oz condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a bowl, coat stuffing in melted butter and set aside.
  2. In another large bowl, add shredded chicken, 1/2 cup shredded cheese, soup, zucchinis, 1/2 of the stuffing/butter mixture, and sour cream and mix well.
  3. Smooth mixture into a 9x13 greased baking dish, top and sprinkle with the remaining 1/2 cup shredded cheese, and then the remaining stuffing.
  4. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until stuffing is golden brown on top.
